About kahwa coffee roasters

Kahwa Coffee Roasters believes in making high-quality coffee accessible to everyone. With a strong online and offline presence, Kahwa offers a rich coffee experience that resonates with both customers and potential franchisees. The journey began when founders Raphael and Sarah Perrier aimed to create the best coffee company in the Tampa Bay area. Now, with over 15 locations, 1000+ wholesale partners, and a significant online presence, Kahwa offers a unique opportunity for entrepreneurs.
